What is the purpose of the figure and figcaption elements?

figure: Used to group multimedia elements such as images, videos, graphics, etc. along with its description or title.
figcaption: Used within the figure element to provide a description or title for the media contained within figure.
In short, figure and figcaption are HTML elements used together to organize and describe multimedia elements on a web page.
